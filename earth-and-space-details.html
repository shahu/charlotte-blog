🌍 Earth and Space: AP Courses & Resources

📚 Related AP Courses

  • AP Environmental Science: Unit 1 (The Living World: Ecosystems), Unit 4 (Earth Systems and Resources), Unit 8 (Aquatic and Terrestrial Pollution)
  • AP Physics: Universal Gravitation, Light Properties

🔬 Specific Fundamental Knowledge Points

  • Earth's Systems (地球系统): Study atmosphere (大气层), hydrosphere (水圈), and geological processes (地质过程) like plate tectonics (板块构造) (AP Environmental Science)
  • Water Cycle (水循环): Learn evaporation (蒸发), condensation (凝结), precipitation (降水) and their environmental roles (AP Environmental Science)
  • Universal Gravitation (万有引力): Understand Newton's law: F = G(m₁m₂)/r² governing planetary motion (行星运动) (AP Physics)
  • Solar System (太阳系): Explore planetary components (行星组成) and celestial phenomena (天体现象) through physics principles (AP Physics)

📖 Detailed Analysis and Examples

🌍 Earth's Systems (地球系统)

Example: Plate tectonics (板块构造) explains earthquakes (地震) and mountain formation (山脉形成). The Pacific Ring of Fire (太平洋火环) shows how tectonic boundaries (构造边界) create volcanic activity (火山活动).

💧 Water Cycle (水循环)

Analysis: Solar energy (太阳能) drives evaporation (蒸发) from oceans (海洋) → water vapor (水蒸气) rises and cools → condenses (凝结) into clouds (云) → precipitation (降水) returns water to Earth.

🪐 Universal Gravitation (万有引力)

Example: Earth's gravity (地球引力) (9.8 m/s²) keeps the Moon (月球) in orbit (轨道). The Moon's weaker gravity (1.6 m/s²) allows astronauts (宇航员) to jump higher.

☀️ Solar System (太阳系)

Analysis: Inner planets (内行星) (rocky 岩石质) vs outer planets (外行星) (gas giants 气态巨行星). Distance from Sun (距离太阳) determines temperature (温度) and atmospheric composition (大气组成).
