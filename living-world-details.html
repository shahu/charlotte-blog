🧬 The Living World: AP Courses & Resources

📚 Related AP Courses

  • AP Biology: Unit 1 (Chemistry of Life), Unit 2 (Cell Structure and Function), Unit 4 (Cell Communication and Cell Cycle), Unit 7 (Natural Selection), Unit 8 (Ecology)

🔬 Specific Fundamental Knowledge Points

  • Cell Structure (细胞结构): Learn organelles (细胞器) like mitochondria (线粒体) (powerhouse) and chloroplasts (叶绿体) (photosynthesis 光合作用) in AP Biology
  • DNA Processes (DNA过程): Study DNA replication (DNA复制), transcription (转录) (DNA to RNA), and translation (翻译) (RNA to proteins 蛋白质) in AP Biology
  • Natural Selection (自然选择): Understand how favorable traits (有利性状) become more common in populations (种群) over time (AP Biology)
  • Ecosystem Dynamics (生态系统动力学): Explore energy flow (能量流动), food webs (食物网), and human environmental impact (人类环境影响) (AP Biology)

📖 Detailed Analysis and Examples

🧬 Cell Organelles (细胞器)

Example: Mitochondria (线粒体) are like power plants - they convert glucose (葡萄糖) into ATP energy (ATP能量). Plant cells (植物细胞) also have chloroplasts (叶绿体) that capture sunlight (阳光) for photosynthesis (光合作用).

🧪 DNA to Protein (DNA到蛋白质)

Analysis: DNA → RNA → Protein (蛋白质). DNA is transcribed (转录) into mRNA in the nucleus (细胞核), then mRNA is translated (翻译) into proteins at ribosomes (核糖体) in the cytoplasm (细胞质).

🦋 Natural Selection (自然选择)

Example: Peppered moths (胡椒蛾) in England: dark moths (深色蛾) survived better during industrial pollution (工业污染), but light moths (浅色蛾) returned when pollution decreased.

🌿 Energy Flow (能量流动)

Analysis: Energy flows from producers (生产者) (plants 植物) → primary consumers (初级消费者) (herbivores 草食动物) → secondary consumers (次级消费者) (carnivores 肉食动物). Only ~10% of energy transfers between levels.
